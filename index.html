About Company

PT SENS is engaged in the distribution of industrial engine lubricants that meet and exceed international standards. Our company serves manufacturing, transportation, heavy equipment, shipping, and mining industries.

Vision & Mission

Vision: To be the most effective and efficient solution provider for global industry needs.

Mission:

  • Providing a variety of products to meet all industrial needs
  • Meeting international certification standards
  • Prioritizing service and customer satisfaction
  • Fast and accurate need fulfillment
  • Enhancing client operational performance
  • Cost efficiency for clients

Main Values

  • Focus on Consumers: Long-term, effective communication and tailored solutions
  • Quality Standards: Rigorously tested products meeting international standards
  • Innovation: Constantly evolving with industry needs
  • Integrity: Honesty, transparency, and reliability in all dealings

Our Products

  • Heavy Machinery: Lubricants for excavators, loaders, cranes, etc.
  • Manufacturing: Supports production machinery with minimal downtime
  • Shipping: Marine-grade lubricants for extreme environments
  • Iron Processing: Lubricants for cutting, pressing, forming metals
  • Mining: Specialized lubricants for extreme conditions
