About Company

PT SENS is a company engaged in the distribution of industrial engine lubricants that meet and exceed international quality standards. Committed to providing solutions for industrial engine performance, maintaining engine age and durability, achieving optimum performance at an efficient cost for the Company.

Vision & Mission

Vision: To be the most effective and efficient solution provider for global industry needs.

Mission:

  • Providing a variety of products to meet all industrial needs
  • Meet international certification standards and maintain product quality
  • Priority of service and customer satisfaction
  • Fast and precise fulfillment of needs
  • Maintaining the effectiveness of the client company's operational performance
  • Cost efficiency for client Companies

Main Values

  • Focus on consumers: We maintain good and long-term relationships with consumers to provide the most effective and efficient solutions.
  • Quality standards: Every product has gone through strict testing to meet international standards.
  • Innovation: We innovate and adapt to industry needs.
  • Integrity: We uphold honesty, responsibility, and transparency.

Our Products

  • Heavy Machinery: Lubricants for construction and mining equipment.
  • Manufacturing: Lubricants for production machines to reduce downtime.
  • Shipping and Maritime: High-protection lubricants against corrosion and moisture.
  • Iron Processing: Lubricants for metal cutting, pressing, and forming.
  • Mining: Specialized lubricants for extreme conditions.
